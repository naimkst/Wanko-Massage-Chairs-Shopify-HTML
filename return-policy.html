Return Policy

At Wanko Massage Chairs, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied, we offer a straightforward return policy to ensure a smooth process. Please read the following policy carefully to understand our terms and conditions.

Eligibility for Returns

  • Return Window: You may return your massage chair within 30 days of the delivery date for a refund or exchange.
  • Condition: The item must be in its original condition, unused, and in the original packaging. Returns that do not meet these conditions may be subject to refusal or additional fees.
  • Proof of Purchase: A valid proof of purchase is required for all returns and exchanges.

Return Process

  • Request a Return Authorization: Contact our customer service team at cs@wankomassagechairs.com or call us at 510 365 1775 within 30 days of delivery to request a Return Authorization (RA) number. Include your order number, reason for the return, and any supporting documentation or photos.
  • Prepare Your Return: Once you receive your RA number, carefully repack the item in its original packaging. Clearly write the RA number on the outside of the package.
  • Arrange for Return Shipping: You are responsible for return shipping costs. We recommend using a trackable shipping service and purchasing shipping insurance for your protection. Wanko Massage Chairs is not responsible for items lost or damaged during return shipping.
  • Return Inspection: Upon receiving your return, our team will inspect the item to ensure it meets the return conditions. This process may take up to 5 business days.

Refunds and Restocking Fees

  • Refund Processing: Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days after the return inspection is complete. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used at the time of purchase.
  • Restocking Fee: A restocking fee of 15% of the purchase price will be deducted from your refund to cover inspection, repackaging, and restocking costs.
  • Original Shipping Costs: Original shipping costs are non-refundable and will be deducted from the refund amount.

Exchanges

  • Exchanges: If you wish to exchange your massage chair for a different model, please follow the return process to return the original item. Once we receive and inspect the return, we will issue a refund (minus any applicable fees), and you can place a new order for the desired model.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Final Sale Items: Items marked as "Final Sale" are not eligible for return or exchange.
  • Customized or Personalized Items: Customized or personalized massage chairs are not eligible for return or exchange unless defective or damaged upon arrival.

Damaged or Defective Items

  • Inspection: Please inspect your massage chair upon delivery. If you notice any damage or defects, notify the delivery personnel immediately and contact our customer service team within 48 hours.
  • Claims: To file a claim for a damaged or defective item, please contact our customer service team with your order number, photos of the damage or defect, and a description of the issue. We will arrange for a repair, replacement, or refund at no additional cost to you.

Contact Us

  • If you have any questions or need assistance with your return, please contact our customer service team at cs@wankomassagechairs.com or call us at 510 365 1775. We are here to help!
